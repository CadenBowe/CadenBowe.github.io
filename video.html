How to Embed a Video into a Website

Step 1: Choose a Video Hosting Service

  1. Identify the video you want to embed.
  2. Ensure the video is publicly accessible or has the necessary permissions for sharing.

Step 2: Locate the Embed Code

For YouTube:

  1. Go to the video on YouTube.
  2. Click on the Share button below the video.
  3. Select Embed from the sharing options.
  4. Copy the HTML embed code.
